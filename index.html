Security Precautions

Follow these best practices to protect your systems and data from cyber threats.

Use strong, unique passwords for every account.
Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) wherever possible.
Keep all software and operating systems updated.
Be cautious of suspicious emails and links (avoid phishing).
Install and regularly update antivirus and anti-malware software.
Back up important data regularly to secure locations.
Use secure Wi-Fi networks and avoid public Wi-Fi for sensitive activities.
Limit sharing of personal information on social media.
Review app permissions and only install trusted applications.
Monitor your accounts and credit reports for unusual activity.
